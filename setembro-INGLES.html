HEALTH

Yellow September – Suicide Prevention

The color represents life, light, and hope. It's the largest mental health campaign in the country. Objectives: Reduce stigma Encourage dialogue about depression, anxiety, and emotional suffering Promote support channels such as 188 (CVV) It's a month dedicated to saving lives through information and support.

Red September – Heart Health

Red symbolizes the heart itself. It addresses the prevention of cardiovascular diseases: Hypertension Heart attack Stroke It encourages healthy habits, physical activity, and cholesterol/blood pressure control.

Green September – Organ Donation

Green represents life, rebirth, and solidarity. The month emphasizes: The importance of communicating your wish to be an organ donor to your family Reducing the transplant waiting list Clarifying myths about donation

SOCIAL

In some states, Green September is also used for campaigns focusing on: Inclusion Accessibility Rights of people with disabilities Discussing topics such as: Architectural barriers Prejudice Inclusion in the job market

ENVIRONMENTAL

September is often informally associated with: Forest protection campaigns Environmental education It is also Tree Month (September 21st), which reinforces preservation and reforestation efforts.
