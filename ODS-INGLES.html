SDG

It aims to end poverty in all its forms, ensuring that all people have access to basic rights such as income, essential services, social protection, and equal opportunities. It also seeks to strengthen policies to reduce vulnerabilities to economic, social, and environmental crises.
It promotes healthy lives and well-being for all at all ages. It includes goals such as reducing child and maternal mortality, combating communicable diseases (such as HIV and tuberculosis), expanding access to quality healthcare services, and promoting mental well-being.
It aims to protect, restore, and promote the sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems. It involves conserving forests, combating deforestation, protecting biodiversity, restoring degraded soils, and addressing desertification, ensuring ecological balance and the preservation of life on Earth.
