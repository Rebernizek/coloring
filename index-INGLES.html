Colors that Mark
the Months

This set of months highlights essential causes for society: July, focusing on health and the encouragement of physical and mental care; August, which represents social actions, promoting solidarity and community support; and September, dedicated to environmental awareness, reinforcing the importance of preservation and sustainability. Together, they form a period of reflection and action for the well-being of individuals, society, and the planet.
