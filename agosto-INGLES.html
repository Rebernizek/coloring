Golden August â€“ Breastfeeding

The color gold symbolizes the "gold standard" of breastfeeding. The campaign reinforces that breast milk: Strengthens the baby's immune system Reduces the risk of allergies, infections, and obesity in the future Stimulates the emotional bond between mother and child Protects women's health, reducing the risk of breast and ovarian cancer The entire month encourages: Support spaces for breastfeeding Training for healthcare professionals Protection of the rights of working mothers

SOCIAL

Part of the Golden August movement involves social issues such as: The right to breastfeed in public places Support for single mothers Promotion of public policies for maternity leave This places the topic within the social sphere as well as the health sphere.

ENVIRONMENTAL

August doesn't have an official environmental color, however: Several regional campaigns focus on combating wildfires. Attention increases due to the dry season in Brazil. Many institutions associate environmental actions with August because of the worsening air quality during this period.
